LumberJack

The goal of LumberJack is to provide you with the means of staying on top of their health and fitness plans through ongoing social interaction. Lumberjack will function as a cross-platform networking application for people to come together with the common interest of healthy living. Through Lumberjack, people will be able to share nutritional information and exercise routines with the goal of staying physically active and maintaining a healthy nutritional diet.
